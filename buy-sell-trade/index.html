Buying Cards

🛒

Sell Your Cards

Selling to Bud

Looking to move cards? Bud buys singles, collections, binders, and bulk commons. No appointment needed at shows — just bring what you've got. For large lots, reach out ahead of time so Bud can prepare a fair, researched offer.

What Bud Buys

✅ Pokémon singles — vintage, modern, alt arts, secret rares
✅ Magic: The Gathering — reserved list, Commander staples, format playables
✅ Yu-Gi-Oh! — 1st edition vintage, meta singles, collections
✅ One Piece TCG — English and Japanese
✅ Lorcana — singles and sealed
✅ Sealed product — booster boxes, ETBs, booster packs
✅ Bulk commons/uncommons — by the thousand

The Selling Process

1

Contact or Bring Cards

DM on social media with a photo/list, or bring cards directly to a show. No appointment needed for small lots.

2

Cards Are Reviewed

Bud evaluates condition and cross-references PriceCharting (TCGPlayer & eBay recently sold data) to build an offer.

3

Offer Made — No Pressure

You'll receive a transparent, itemized offer. No pressure to accept. The offer is explained and you're free to walk away.

4

Get Paid Immediately

Cash at shows. Digital payments (Venmo @budcollectibles, Zelle, PayPal G&S) within 24 hours for online transactions.

Trade Your Cards

Trading with Bud

Trading is one of the best parts of this hobby. Bud is always open to fair trades — whether you're looking to upgrade, move to a different game, or swap a collection you've outgrown.

Trade Preferences

📊

Valuation Method

PriceCharting market price (TCGPlayer & eBay recently sold data) is used as the base for all trade valuations. Both sides of the trade are evaluated using the same standard — no double standards.

🎯

What Bud Trades For

High-demand singles in any TCG, graded cards, sealed product, vintage items, and bulk collections. If you have it, ask — the answer is usually yes.

⚖️

Trade Add-Ons

If a trade isn't perfectly even, cash can bridge the gap on either side. No trade should feel lopsided — if it does, something gets adjusted.

The Trade Process

1

Reach Out

DM on Instagram or X (Twitter) with what you have and what you're looking for. Or bring cards to a show.

2

Cards Are Valued

Both sides pull up PriceCharting market values (TCGPlayer & eBay recently sold data) together. Transparency is key — Bud will show you exactly what reference is being used.

3

Deal or Adjust

If values line up, great. If not, cards or cash are added to even things out. Nobody walks away feeling shortchanged.

4

Cards Swap Hands

Both parties inspect their received cards before the trade finalizes. Once exchanged, trades are complete — inspect carefully before agreeing.

Delivery

Shipping Info

📍 Domestic Shipping

Ships to all US addresses via USPS or UPS. Orders typically ship within 1–3 business days of payment confirmation.

🌍 International

Not available at this time. US-only shipping. Check back in the future as this may change.

🎁 Shipping Rates

PWE (Plain White Envelope): $1.50 — low-cost option for singles. BMWT (Bubble Mailer w/ Tracking): $5 — medium-cost option for singles and small bulk. Boxed: $10–15 — for sealed product & bulk-buys. Free tracked shipping on orders over $100.

🔒 Packaging

PWE: singles sleeved, in a top loader taped. BMWT: sleeved in a top loader, put into a team bag, wrapped in bubble wrap. Sealed Product or Bulk-Buys: wrapped in bubble wrap, boxed.

📋 Tracking

BMWT and Boxed shipments include a tracking number sent via the same contact method used to arrange the sale — typically within 24 hours of carrier drop-off. PWE orders are untracked and shipped at the buyer's accepted risk. Once tracking shows "Delivered," the shipment is considered complete on our end.

⚠️ Lost, Stolen, or Damage Claims

Cards (Singles & Bulk) and Sealed Products are photographed before shipping. If any arrive damaged due to shipping, contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos for resolution. Ownership of packages turned over to USPS and UPS transfers to the buyer. We are not responsible for stolen, lost, held, or damaged packages.

Large Collections & Lots

Bulk Buying

Have hundreds or thousands of cards to move? Bud buys bulk lots — no cherry-picking required on your end. Bring the whole collection and get a fair offer for everything.

What Counts as Bulk?

📦 Full binder collections (50–1000+ cards)
📦 Commons/uncommons by the thousand
📦 Mixed TCG collections
📦 Parent/grandparent estate collections
📦 "I found these in a box" situations
📦 Full sealed cases or lot purchases

Bulk Process

1

Send Photos First

For large lots, send photos of the best cards and an approximate count via DM or email. This helps Bud prepare a realistic ballpark before you come out.

2

Bring to a Show (or arrange pickup)

For very large collections, Bud may be able to arrange local pickup in the Chicagoland area. Shows are usually the most convenient meeting point.

3

Full Review & Offer

Bud reviews the full lot — high-value cards are identified and priced individually. The remainder is valued as bulk. An itemized breakdown can be provided.

4

Same-Day Payment

Cash at shows for immediate payment. For very large collections, partial payment upfront and remainder within 48 hours can be arranged.
